Episode 1: Documentation

There is a lot of information and data about GBA emulation online. Most sources are incomplete or inaccurate. Here are the good ones:

- GBATek, a.k.a. the bible, describes GBA and DS hardware.
- CowBite specs, describing in detail the GBA CPU and Memory.
- ARM7tdmi reference manual, the GBA's CPU specs.
- Deku's sound tutorial and The Audio Advance, focusing on the GBA sound system.
- Tonc, a complete guide of GBA game development, with many examples.
- GBAnews's public domain ROMS collection.

(My online emulator will provide these ROMs plus TONC and DevKitARM examples)

I'll be back when I have read all this stuff.
